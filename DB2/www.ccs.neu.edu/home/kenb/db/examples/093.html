Sample Exercise

Normalize the following relations when each one satisfies the specified functional dependencies. Decompose into BCNF if possible. If not, then decompose into 3NF. Show your work. In particular, show the list of all candidate keys of the original relation. To compute the candidate keys, use the discussion in Candidate Keys. In your decomposition show the attributes in each relation and the primary key of each relation.

  1. The relation is ABCDE
    • AB→C
    • B→E
    • C→A
  2. The relation is ABCDEF
    • C→BE
    • BDF→C
    • E→F
  3. The relation is ABCDEF
    • B→CD
    • ACF→D
    • C→D
  4. The relation is ABCDEFG
    • AEF→D
    • D→A
    • G→C
    • AB→D
  5. The relation is ABCDEFG
    • CDFG→A
    • D→F
    • C→E
    • ACD→B
    • AF→D
  6. The relation is ABCDEFGH
    • ADF→G
    • CEF→H
    • F→G
    • D→B
    • CFH→A
  7. The relation is ABCDEFGH
    • ABGH→D
    • C→B
    • CF→G
    • E→G
    • ACH→D
    • AG→D
