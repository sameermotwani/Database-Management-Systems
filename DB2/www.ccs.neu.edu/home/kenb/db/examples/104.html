Sample Exercise

Design a database for bands. Persons have names and a date of birth. Each person has a unique integer identifier, and may belong to bands. A band has a unique name, has members, and may have a style. A person may play instruments. An instrument has a name and may have a musical key. A string instrument has a number of strings, a brass instrument has a number of valves. An instrument can also be a percussion instrument.
