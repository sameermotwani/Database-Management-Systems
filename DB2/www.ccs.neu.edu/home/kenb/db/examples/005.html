Sample Exercise

Design a database for keeping track of tennis tournament results. A tournament has a name and period of time when it occurs. A tournament has one or more events, each specifying the kind of player that can participate in the event, e.g., "Men's Singles". An event has at least two players. A player may be a single person or two persons. A player may have a seed for the event. Each person has a name. Players compete against other players in an event. The status of a competition has three possibilities: a win for one of the two players or the result is not yet known.

Show your design as a UML diagram. Show all cardinality constraints on both associations and attributes.
