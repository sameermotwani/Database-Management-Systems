Sample Exercise

For the following relations and functional dependencies:

  1. Show the list of all candidate keys of the original relation.
  2. Decompose into BCNF if possible. Show your work.
  3. If the relation cannot be decomposed into BCNF, then decompose into 3NF.
  4. In your decomposition show the attributes in each relation and the primary key of each relation.
To compute the candidate keys, use the discussion in Candidate Keys.

  1. The relation is ABCDE
    • DE→B
    • A→BE
    • E→A
  2. The relation is ABCDE
    • BD→A
    • C→A
    • A→C
    • B→D
  3. The relation is ABCDEF
    • AC→E
    • AE→B
    • BCE→A
    • ABE→F
  4. The relation is ABCDEF
    • F→B
    • BDE→A
    • E→D
    • BCD→E
  5. The relation is ABCDEFG
    • AEF→D
    • A→C
    • CG→F
    • DE→F
  6. The relation is ABCDEFG
    • B→F
    • D→A
    • G→C
    • CG→A
  7. The relation is ABCDEFGH
    • B→D
    • D→A
    • E→D
    • EGH→B
  8. The relation is ABCDEFGH
    • AGH→B
    • CE→B
    • B→G
    • ABCG→F
